Knight Platformer

This is my very first game on GoDot. I carefully followed a tutorial by YouTuber Brackeys to help dive into the world of GoDot. After going over the basics for this game, I was able to fully customize the level design. This helped with my understanding of level creation and how some very minute details can effect how the player plays like, getting the hit boxes and collision boxed just right so that there isn't any strange bugs going on, or play testing each part of the level so that the player has the best experience (but in this case the most frustrating one). All assets were pulled from his video "How to make a video game"
